Context & Scale

OfficeMax is a leading omnichannel retailer with >1M monthly site sessions, 828K email subscribers, ~4,200 active SKUs across 14 categories, and annual net revenue projections around MX$172M. I designed and owned the full e-commerce KPI framework — 125 metrics across customer journey, marketing, operations and financial pillars.

Challenge

Multiple product categories, multiple brand partners (HP, Brother, Crayola, Henkel), email as undifferentiated mass blast, paid media without ROI discipline, and 60%+ digital-only buyers during seasonal peaks — all requiring real-time coordination under tight campaign timelines.

🔍
Search & Social Optimization

📧
Email Segmentation Rebuild
Moved from mass blasts to behavioral segmentation by purchase history, category interest and engagement (via Mailup). Grew subscriber base from 197K → 828K. Improved open rate +18%, CTR +12%.
🤝
Brand Co-Investment
Negotiated commercial agreements with HP, Brother, Crayola and Henkel to fund promotional inventory expansion and co-marketing budgets.
📊
125-KPI WBR Framework
Designed and implemented a weekly business review system covering customer journey, marketing effectiveness, inventory health, logistics SLA and financial profitability across 14 product categories.
